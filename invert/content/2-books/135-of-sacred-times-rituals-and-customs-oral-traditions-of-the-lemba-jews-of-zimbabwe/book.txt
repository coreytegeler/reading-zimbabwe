Synopsis: *Of Sacred Times, Rituals, and Customs: Oral Traditions of the Lemba Jews of Zimbabwe* is an e-book by Zimbabwean scholar Rabson Wuriga about the Lemba Jewish community. Dr. Wuriga, a scholar and a member of the Lemba community of Zimbabwe, shares his interviews with Lemba elders and his analysis of Lemba Judaic customs. Research was funded by the Institute of Jewish and Community Research.
