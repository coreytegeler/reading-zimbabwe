Title: David Simmons

----

Text: David Simmons is an associate professor with a dual position in the Department of Anthropology and the Department of Health Promotion, Education, and Behavior at the University of South Carolina. As a cultural anthropologist, he studies health and healing practices as they intersect with various fields of power in a range of historical and contemporary contexts. His topical specializations are in infectious disease (specifically HIV/AIDS), vernacular forms of medicine, and social justice.
